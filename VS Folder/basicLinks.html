Some of my Favorite Sites

Wikipedia

One of my favorite websites is called wikipedia. This terrific site allows users to enter encyclopedia definitions. Over time, the entries can be as reliable as a commercial encyclopedia, and a lot more complete.

Dummies

You can find a lot about upcoming and current Dummies books at www.dummies.com.

PopURLs

If you want to know what is happening on the internet today, check out popurls.com. This site aggregates a bunch of social networking sites.
